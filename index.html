Civil War & Revolutionary War Tours

Battlear sits at the crossroads of American history. Just miles from the environs of Washington DC’s defenses, Monocacy, Antietam, Gettysburg, and nestled along the historic Catoctin Mountains, we're your base for exploring Maryland’s role in both the Civil War and the American Revolutionary War.

Our tours are personal, tailored to your interests, and collaborative. We share hidden stories, local legends, and mysteries—including an unexplained cannonball dent in Sharpsburg. We’re also building a project to collect and share guests’ own ancestral connections to these wars.
